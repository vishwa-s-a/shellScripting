* Arithmetic operators:
+,-,/,%,*,
= is for assignment
== is for equality
!= is for non-equality

* Relational operators
-eq : equal
-ne : not equal
-lt : less than
-le : less than or equal
-gt : greater than
-ge : greater than or equal


* Boolean operators
! : logical negation
-o : logical or
-a : logical and
